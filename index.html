The Problem

California contractors are exposed. Every single day.

📄
Unlicensed Templates
Most contractors are still using handwritten contracts or random PDFs that don't comply with Business & Professions Code §7159. They don't know what they're missing.
⚖️
Compliance is a Minefield
B&P 7159 mandates specific font sizes, statutory language, cancellation periods, and payment caps. One wrong clause voids the contract entirely.
💸
Disputes Kill Businesses
A non-compliant HIC contract can't be enforced in court. Contractors lose mechanic's lien rights and are exposed to license suspension by the CSLB.
"The contractor who wins disputes has the better contract. Most contractors don't even know what's required."
— The gap BuildRight closes.

How It Works

Three steps from job site to signed, compliant contract.

👤
Step 1 — Profile Setup
Contractor enters business info once: license #, insurance carrier, logo, trade type. Every future contract auto-fills from their profile. CSLB # validated on entry.
📋
Step 2 — Fill the Form
Step-by-step guided HIC form. Scope, materials, exclusions, dates, payment schedule. Compliance rules enforced in real time. AI Scope Writer available in Phase 2.
📤
Step 3 — Send & Sign
Tap Send. Customer gets a branded email with signing link. Signs on their phone. Both parties receive the signed PDF. Contract saved automatically.
